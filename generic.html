About Us

Hello! I’m Mark Torre, a recent graduate from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where I earned my degree in Sociology. My academic and professional journey has equipped me with a robust foundation in visual storytelling, strategic marketing, and project management. My passion lies in creating compelling content that resonates deeply with audiences. I have developed a unique videography style that combines hundreds of thousands of meticulously curated photos to create stop-motion videos. This technique captures life’s vivid moments in unparalleled detail, whether it’s an individual achieving a milestone, a love story unfolding, a joyous baby shower, a dynamic political campaign, or the profound sacrifices of soldiers. The limitless potential of this medium is why my YouTube channel is aptly named @Captured, with the hashtag #storyoflife encapsulating my work. Filmmaking is my ultimate passion. Through this medium, I aim to capture and preserve the essence of life’s fleeting moments. I firmly believe that videos and photos are the closest things we have to time travel. Memories can be loosely stored in our minds or cataloged as hard documentation forever—the choice is ours.
